The Shift

B2B buying is being automated. Faster than you think.

The way companies discover, evaluate, and purchase software is fundamentally changing. AI agents — not humans — are becoming the first (and sometimes only) touchpoint.

01

AI Search Is the New Top of Funnel

Founders and ops teams are asking ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude for vendor recommendations instead of Googling. If your product isn't in the AI's answer, you don't exist.

02

Personal AI Agents Are Evaluating You

Tools like OpenClaw and custom GPTs are being used by small teams to research, compare, and shortlist B2B tools. They read your docs, test your API, and score you — without a human ever visiting your site.

03

Agentic Procurement Is Coming

Autonomous agents will handle entire purchase workflows end-to-end — from discovery to vendor negotiation to contract signing. The companies who are ready for this will win the next decade.
